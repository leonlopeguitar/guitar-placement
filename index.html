About the lessons

A system built for real progress

I'm Nicolás, a guitarist and composer based in Colombia. I've taught students across Australia, the UK, and beyond — online, 1-on-1, in English.

Every student starts with a structured placement to find exactly where they are. Then we build from there — level by level, song by song — with a clear path forward at every step.

No generic YouTube tutorials. No wasted lessons. Just a curriculum that grows with you.
